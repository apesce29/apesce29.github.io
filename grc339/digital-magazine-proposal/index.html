Introduction

Shore Brew

Shore Brew is a magazine for coffee lovers who feel most at home by the ocean. It explores cafes that line California’s coast from San Diego to Santa Cruz. From tiny surf town cafés to bigger roasters by the water, each spot has its own vibe and story. This is a place to share those atmospheres, the drinks we love, and the communities that bring them to life. Shore Brew celebrates the way coffee connects people and shares stories, photos, and places that make coastal coffee culture so unique.
